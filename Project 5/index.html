Unison Square Garden (stylized as UNISON SQUARE GARDEN, or USG) is a Japanese pop rock band signed to Toy's Factory. The band includes Kosuke Saito (vocals/guitar), Tomoya Tabuchi (bass/backing vocals), and Takao Suzuki (drums).

Style and Influence:
  • Their music combines catchy pop-rock elements with intricate compositions, creating a distinct, vibrant sound.
  • Known for their technical skill and versatility, they experiment with various genres within their discography.

    • They are well-known for their unique fusion of energetic rock, pop melodies, and intricate musical arrangements. The band has gained significant popularity both in Japan and internationally, partly due to their contributions to anime soundtracks.
